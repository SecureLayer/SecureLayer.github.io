## Privacy Policy

Effective date: December 28, 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how this simple landing page collects, uses, and protects information. It applies only to data collected through this website.

### Information we collect
- **Automatically:** non-identifying technical data such as anonymized IP (removed before any external processing), browser type, device type, pages visited, referrer, and timestamps via server logs.
- **Voluntarily:** contact details or messages you submit via a contact form or email (name, email, message).
- **Cookies & similar:** minimal non‑persistent cookies or local storage only when necessary for site functionality or analytics; no tracking across other sites.

### Purpose of processing
- To respond to inquiries sent via the contact form or email.
- To operate, maintain, and improve the website.
- To produce aggregated, anonymized metrics about site usage.

### Legal basis
- Consent for contact form submissions and optional cookies.
- Legitimate interests for site operation and basic analytics.

### Data sharing and third parties
- We do not sell or rent personal data.
- Minimal data may be shared with trusted third‑party service providers (hosting, email delivery, analytics) strictly to perform services for the site. Any shared data is limited to what is necessary.
- Where applicable, data sent to third parties is minimized and anonymized.

### Data retention
- Contact messages are kept until the request is resolved or for a maximum of 2 years unless you request deletion.
- Logs and analytics data are retained in aggregated or anonymized form; raw logs are retained only as needed for operations and security (maximum 90 days).

### Security
- Reasonable administrative, technical, and physical measures are in place to protect data. No system is fully secure; we follow best practices to reduce risk.

### Your rights
- You can request access, correction, deletion, or portability of your personal data submitted via contact form or email.
- You can withdraw consent for any optional cookies.
- To exercise rights, contact the email address below. Verification may be required.

### International transfers
- If data is transferred outside your country, we take steps to maintain appropriate protections consistent with applicable law.

### Children
- This site is not directed to children under 16. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children.

### Changes to this policy
- We may update this policy. The effective date above will reflect the latest version.
